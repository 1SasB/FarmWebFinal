About Us

Previous Next

Farmwealth is a web application platform that focuses on Livestock and Aquaculture with a technology that enable investors to invest and monitor their livestock & Aquaculture business and also help smallholder farmer secure capital to facilities their growth for a more profitable business. Farmwealth develops a funding and management web and Mobile application for livestock and aquaculture.

Farm Wealth uses technology to connect smallholder famers with much- needed investment across Ghana, helping to close the agriculture financing gap and connecting urban investors with agriculture. Farm Wealth serves as a conduit between small- scale farmers in need of capital and retail investors wishing to invest in the sector.

Our platform helps our clients to invest in tangible, growing assets at the click of a button. Innovation is at the heart of what we do and we aim to expand our product rang and to become the foremost tech- driven platform for livestock investment.

Our investment options include Pig, Goat, Catfish and Cattle
